RosettaSciIO v0.0
=================

- RosettaSciIO was split out of the `HyperSpy repository 
  <https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy>`_ on July 23, 2022. The IO-plugins
  and related functions so far developed in HyperSpy were moved to this
  new repository.
